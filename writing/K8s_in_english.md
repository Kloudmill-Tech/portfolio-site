# K8 in Plain English

----
Kubernetes or K8s for short is an open source container orchestration platform. Let's simplify this broad subject. First, think of containers as delivery packages from your favorite online store. Packages contain all the items in your order (applications) and the delivery company (K8s) is responsible for ==managing the transport and safety of said packages==
  K8s is the delivery company in our example; which is in charge of managing our packages seecurly. K8s are used in orchestrating thousands of packages or applications within their lifecyle. Without K8s, how can the online store assure packages are being shipped and received properly. Let's breakdown the following:  
  
- What are containers?
- What are VMs?
- What is Orchestration?

## Containers Explained

----
:memo: Containers are lightweight packages that contain an applications dependices, code, drivers.

## VMs Explained

----
:memo: Virtural Machines or VMs are a full operating system with drivers, applications, graphics cards and more. VMs take more resources on their host and are not as scalable compared to containers

## Orchestration Explained

----
:memo: Orchestration refers the management of several VMs or Containers simulataneously. Its a method of clicking once and deploying a change to many endpoints.

## Summary

K8s is used and a management or orchestration tool for contaniners on large enterprise scale. Organizations of all sixe utlize k8s to streamline delivery of applications packaged in containers, which allow for shorter time to market and feature updates.
